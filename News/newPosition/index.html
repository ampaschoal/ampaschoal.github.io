    New Position as Assistant Professor: University of Campinas

    October 11th, 2022

    I am very happy to announce that I was recently approved as an Assistant Professor at the University of Campinas (UNICAMP), where I'll be joining the Physics Institute "Gleb Wataghin". I'm really excited to start my own lab, which will also be part of the Neurophysics group. I'm expected to start my activities there in January 2023.

    As an assistant professor, I intend to strengthen my research line of quantitative MRI, especially with the development and application of advanced ASL methods. On the other hand, I'm also excited to start some multimodal studies by combining my MRI results with other those from other techniques (e.g. NIRS and EEG) in a collaboration with the Neurophysics group.

    About UNICAMP

    UNICAMP is a very important Brazilian university, located in Campinas, which is a big city of the Sao Paulo State, one hour driving from Sao Paulo city. In its beautiful campus, you can find a really big range of undergraduate and graduate courses in humanities, exact and biological Sciences.

    The UNICAMP campus also encopasses the Clinical Hospital of Campinas, a very big and modern School Hospital where I'll be performing my MRI experiments.
