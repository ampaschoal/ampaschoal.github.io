    Open PhD student position

    July 19th, 2023

    A PhD Opportunity with FAPESP Scholarship is open in our lab! The candidate will be associated to the Institute of Physics Gleb Wataghin, of University of Campinas (UNICAMP).

    For this scholarship, the student will work on the development of methods for the acquisition and processing of magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) with the aim of mapping the mechanisms of the brain clearance and the functioning of the glymphatic system. It is expected that, by the end of the doctoral period, the student will have acquired extensive knowledge of MRI physics, image processing, and the relationship between blood flow and cerebrospinal fluid with impurity clearance and the glymphatic system.

    The project will be developed in collaboration with the Clinical Hospital of Unicamp, where we will be using a 3T Philips scanner and a 7T Bruker preclinical scanner.

    We are looking for candidates with the following profile:

    • Motivated and interested in advanced MRI developments to address complex questions.
    • Individuals motivated to guide their research with innovative ideas.
    • Interest and/or experience in programming languages, image processing, and MRI physics.
    Interested candidates, please send the following documents to andremp@unicamp.br:
    • CV;
    • Motivation letter;
    • At least one letter of recommendation from a supervisor or former supervisor.
    I am open to discussions for further clarifications.

    Deadline for application: September 15th
